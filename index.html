Concept Overview

Entrepreneurship is a journey often defined by resilience, risk, and reinvention. Cheers to Small Wins is more than a celebration — it is a purpose-driven gathering that recognises the spirit of entrepreneurship while fostering collaboration, growth, and renewed purpose for the year ahead.

Purpose & Objectives
  • Celebrate resilience and inspire renewed hope
  • Facilitate meaningful connections & collaboration
  • Create visibility for brands and entrepreneurs
  • Highlight the human side of entrepreneurship
Strategic Relevance to Corporates

Connect with the heartbeat of South Africa’s economy — its small business sector — while supporting transformation, ESD, and empowerment goals.

Event Highlights

Feel-good performances to close the year.

Target Audience

Small business owners, startup founders, corporate leaders, ESD managers, business support organisations, funders, and development agencies.
